Success can be measured through the change in the amount of visitors after the page is published. Site metrics can be used to attain an individual number of site visits per user as well as keep track of data such as team sign ups using the online forms.

If there is a noticeable increase of fifty percent or more in the amount of signups to the club from the website, the site would be deemed a successful venture.

Target Audience

The sites target audience is more focused to appeal to kids still attending highschool and upwards to thirty years of age. For this website to appeal to this age group, appropriate design should be used to capture the users attention.
